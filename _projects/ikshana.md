**Institute Technical Summer Project** | IIT Bombay

**Role:** Team Lead | **Duration:** May - Aug 2023

- Led a team of four students to develop a successful proof-of-concept; adjudged 2nd among 100+ teams
- Designed and fabricated wearable foot harnesses with haptic feedback for real-time directional guidance
- Developed an Android app and used OpenStreetMap to create an intersection database for campus navigation
